An introduction to pyweek

First let's start off by explaining what pyweek is.
Pyweek is a bi-annual (twice a year) event that is held by the current developers/maintainers of python, pyweek allows users to create a game based of the winning catergory that the contenders choose. There are usually 5 catergories that the contenders can pick from. In pyweek35, the pyweek myself and RangerRhino took part in, the choices were as follows:

•In the shadows
•Las Vegas Nights
•Mass-produced
•Left, right, down
•Re-entry

Mine and RangerRhino's entry

and after casting out votes and waiting for the voting to end the victorious catergory was In the shadows which lead to us making Echoes in the Dark which lead to us getting 2.85/4 and scoring quite well.
